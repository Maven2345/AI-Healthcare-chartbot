[CLINICAL PROTOCOL: MALARIA REBELS]
In northern Namibia (Oshikoto, Ohangwena, Omusati, Kunene), high fever, shivering, and severe headache must be prioritized for rapid diagnostic tests (RDTs) at local state clinics. Local terms: Fiva/Feba (Oshiwambo), |Amaseb (Nama), Omuvarandje (Otjiherero).

[OPERATIONAL PROTOCOL: CLINIC HOURS]
Standard Ministry of Health and Social Services (MoHSS) primary health care centers operate from 08:00 to 17:00. Emergency triage variants are open 24/7.
